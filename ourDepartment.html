The BCA Department

Our department, BCA (Bachelor of Computer Application), is home to a team of three esteemed teachers: Mrs. Anjani Mishra, Mr. Imran Ahmed, and Mr. D.K. Gupta. Their expertise and guidance have been instrumental in shaping our educational journey. We are fortunate to have access to a well-equipped computer lab, equipped with an ample number of computer systems, as well as a Wi-Fi enabled environment. This infrastructure has greatly facilitated our practical learning and hands-on experience, enabling us to explore the depths of computer applications and programming. We are grateful for the resources and support provided by our department, which have contributed to our academic growth and development.
