About My College

HRPG College was established in July 1966 under the affiliation of Deen Dayal Upadhyaya Gorakhpur University. It received permanent affiliation with Deen Dayal Upadhyaya University in 1971 and it is the only grant and aid postgraduate college of Sant Kabir Nagar District. The college is administered by Hiralal Ram Niwas educational society which is registered by the registrar of the society. The college is included in University Grants Commission, New Delhiâ€™s article 2F and 12B and has been accredited by the National Evaluation and Accreditation Council (NAAC). It offers undergraduate and postgraduate courses in Arts, Science, Commerce, and B.Ed. and several employment-focused courses in several fields.

On the day of creation of this web site

On this eventful day of launching our website, we find ourselves amidst the preparations for our sixth-semester exams. We are diligently organizing our files for the upcoming viva and practical examinations. Simultaneously, we are engrossed in working on our major project, applying the programming languages we have acquired during our studies. As the culmination of our efforts nears, we eagerly anticipate completing our Bachelor's degree in Computer Applications (BCA) in just a few days. This milestone marks the culmination of our academic journey, and we are excited to embark on new adventures in the field of technology.


What we did yet ?

As a group of friends, we had an incredible time enjoying the various facilities provided by our college, H.R.P.G College. We engaged in a wide range of activities, including throwing javelin, playing carom and badminton, working out in the gymnasium, and programming in the new computer lab. From the moment we took admission until we obtained our bachelor's degrees in computer applications, we immersed ourselves in learning a plethora of programming languages such as C, C++, SQL, PL/SQL, Java, C#, HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. We also gained proficiency in utilizing numerous software and delved deep into studying computers. Through programming, we had the opportunity to design cars, kites, and even animations. Additionally, we acquired knowledge about the Linux operating system, which is widely used by companies like Google and Facebook. Our studies included understanding how microprocessors operate on data, among many other fascinating subjects.
